Frequently asked questions

Does Solstice replace my calendar?

No — Google Workspace or Outlook stays your source of truth. Solstice syncs both ways and only writes events you've approved.

How does scheduling work across time zones?

Every teammate sets working hours once. Solstice scores candidate slots by how much shared, in-hours, out-of-focus time they consume and proposes the cheapest one — so nobody takes the 6 a.m. call twice in a row.

What happens when my team outgrows the free plan?

Nothing breaks. Existing automations keep running; you just can't add a sixth member or a fourth automation until you upgrade.

Is my calendar data private?

Event contents are encrypted at rest, insights are aggregated per team, and we never sell or share data. Deleting your workspace deletes everything within 30 days.

Can I cancel any time?

Yes. Downgrades take effect at the end of the billing period, and you can export your automations and history first.
